Covid

We take Covid seriously. You may be a healthy, fit cyclist, but you can still easily get Covid. It can make you horribly sick and it can kill you, both of which are very bad for your cycling. However, the scientific consensus seems to be that there is very little risk in cycling with other people. As we learn more about the virus that consensus may change, but for now we think that NoSoX - NC can go forward. We urge everyone to take reasonable precautions: wear a mask when you're with other people indoors and when you're in proximity to others outdoors while not moving. Don't blow your nose onto people behind you in a paceline. Get vaccinated! Finally, sign up on the "Contact" page so that we can send you news if things change and we decide to modify or cancel NoSoX.
